IFIC is the primary collector and provider of essential mutual funds sales data. We serve as the hub for information coming from and going to the Mutual Fund Industry.

FUNCTION

Our role is to manage the collection of data from our contributing organizations, process the information, and provide a set of data that is usable and comparable for research and competitive analysis.

FRAMEWORK

IFIC is the primary source of mutual fund historical and statistical data. IFIC Statistics underwent some changes to the reports and statistics available on the Canadian retail mutual fund industry.
